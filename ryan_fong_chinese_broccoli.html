Ingredients

  • Chinese Broccoli
  • Oyster Sauce
  • Garlic
  • Olive Oil
  • Sugar

Chinese Broccoli

Description

This is a simple recipe for Chinese Broccoli

Directions

  1. Bring pan of 2 cups water to boil
  2. Add Chinese Broccoli and a pinch of sugar, let boil until water is gone
  3. Add olive oil
  4. Stir Fry For 5 minutes, mixing in garlic
  5. Place on serving dish and add a generous amount of oyster sauce
